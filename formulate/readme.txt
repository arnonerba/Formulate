Formulate is a simple contact form for WordPress.

== Description ==

Formulate allows you to add a single contact form to your site to collect a visitor\'s name, email address, and message. You can have the contact form submissions emailed to the blog administrator or to a custom email address. Use of a Google reCAPTCHA is integrated and mandatory.
